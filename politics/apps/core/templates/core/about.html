govmonitor was created out of frustration with the inaccessibility of politics. Policy documents are scattered all over the Internet, making it impossible for most people to work out what parties actually stand for. Even when you've found one, they only list the policies the party wants you to know about; you need to scour news websites to find the rest of them!

By collecting all of this information and making it easy to understand, we hope to simplify politics and help people vote for the parties that best represents their views. We take an evidence-based approach, with the site powered entirely by user-submitted references.
