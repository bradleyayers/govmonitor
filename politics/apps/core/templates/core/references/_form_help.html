Stance

The stance that this reference suggests (e.g. it may indicate that the party supports the issue in question).

URL

Where the reference can be accessed.

  • The reference must be publicly accessible (it must not require a username/password to access).
  • Before posting, ensure that the link works by opening it in a new tab.

Title

An identifying title for the reference (e.g. the title of the news article, the title of the policy document).

Published

When the reference was published (e.g. when the news article was posted, when the policy document was made available).

If you can't determine when it was published, make an estimate or leave the input blank and today's date will be used.

Summary

A brief summary of the reference, outlining the important points or quotes.

  • When quoting, make clear who is speaking and their role (e.g. "Bob Brown, Leader of the Greens").
  • Where possible, include excerpts and quotes verbatim so they can be found using the browser's find function.
  • Markdown formatting is supported:
    • Leave a blank line to end one paragraph and start another.
    • # Header, ## Subheader, etc.
    • [Link](www.example.com)
    • > This is a quote.
> Still a quote.
    • * This is a list.
* Still a list.
